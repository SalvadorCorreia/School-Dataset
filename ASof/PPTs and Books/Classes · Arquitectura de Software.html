Classes

This page contains the material you have to read prior to the classes (lectures and problems). At the end of lectures, an optional mini-test will take place on the subjects of the current lecture and the previous problems class.
The material includes the bibliography of the Software Architecture course, Software Architecture in Practice (SAiP), 4th edition, Documenting Software Architecture (DSA), 2nd edition, System Design Interview – An Insider's Guide: Volume 1 (SDI1), System Design Interview – An Insider's Guide: Volume 2 (SDI2), Microservices Patterns (MP), plus the articles and other sources of information that will be used in the course.

Classes follow the flipped classroom approach. Classes are discussion-driven; read the available material before class.
